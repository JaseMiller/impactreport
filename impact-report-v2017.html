Thank you for signing...

Things are not alright. Therefore, we choose to stand against the status quo. We choose to see the invisible, give a voice to the voiceless, create beauty from ashes, rediscover dignity, restore hope. Circumstances can't erase the fact that every person is of equal value in the eyes of Jesus. We choose to see every person through the eyes of Jesus. We won't separate mission and compassion, because Jesus doesn't. We will pursue transformation in an integrated way that honors the community where we live and work, collaborating with others whenever possible. We choose to work alongside the oppressed and not over them. We identify with the outcasts. We choose to live in a way that reflects Jesus. We believe no one should be invisible and everyone should have access to food, freedom, and forgiveness.

What happens now?

Signing The Declaration entitles you to receive INSIGHT emails...

INSIGHT emails are designed for deep impact in only 1 minute. They prompt us to fix our eyes on Jesus and move with him toward integrated community transformation. Share it with anyone who longs to see lives and communities transformed by the power of God.

We'll be developing more resources and experiences to help you engage in ICT where you are, and as a signer of The Declaration you'll be among the first to know of these opportunities.

Please see all of our INSIGHT articles at www.iteams.us/impact and engage the community in conversation there.

Thank you for standing with the oppressed.


Scott Olson
